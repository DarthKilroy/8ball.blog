## Steps:

1. Go to your **Library** and find **Cyberpunk 2077**
    
2. Press the **Options (☰)** button or **Right-click** the game
    
3. Click **Properties**
    
4. Under the **Launch Options** section, type:
    
    ```
    --launcher-skip
    ```
    
5. Close the window — you're done.
